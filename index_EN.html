Nomad Reline Service

NOMAD RELINE SERVICES - is the first and only international grinding mill refurbishment company based in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia. Our branches, agencies and service teams are strategically spread through New Zealand, Australia, Mongolia, and the Middle East. Establishing ourselves through these regions ensures that we can quickly and efficiently cater to all our clients the world over.
With a large and experienced senior reline team who drive our onsite presence we aim to deliver the fastest, safest, and most efficient service possible.
Ultimately our goal is to provide the very best reline service and experience possible. Fast response, value for money and overall client satisfaction.
To ensure that we can work in harmony with our customers and their workforce we can also provide team members that a bilingual. Key staff are fluent in English, Mongolian, Arabic, Russian, Kyrgyz, Afrikaans, and Fijian.
